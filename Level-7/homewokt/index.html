Titanic

The RMS Titanic was a British passenger liner that sank in the North Atlantic Ocean in 1912.

Quick Facts:

  • Maiden voyage: April 10, 1912
  • Sank: April 15, 1912
  • Passengers: 2,224
  • Survivors: 706
